About the game

An interactive game with an educational twist for children in the age group of 5 - 12.

How to play

The games must be designed to suit the selected age group in terms of colours, type and language. The games must be able to run as stand-alone applications or presented within a website. The games must be fun and intuitive for the child to use.

Read more

The child appreciates immediate feedback when completing a task. Additionally, at the end of a level or series of tasks the child appreciates a score and this must be taken into consideration. The game may be very simple and repeated at different levels or you may choose a more complex game. This is your choice; however, the game must demonstrate use of all the evidence requirements listed in Task 2.
